About Us

Our Mission

Ma'hd-ush-Shaatibiy Qur'an Academy is a dedicated online institute for women, committed to empowering females with the profound and transformative knowledge of the Qur'an. Our goal is to provide a safe space for women to learn, memorize, and implement the teachings of the Qur'an in their everyday lives.

Our Vision

The Qur'an is the sacred word of Allah (SWT), the eternal guidance for humanity. We believe that every woman has the ability to connect with the Qur'an, no matter her background or circumstances. Our vision is to help women of all ages and stages of life to explore, memorize, and understand the Qur'an, in accordance with the authentic teachings of Islam.

Our Values

We are grounded in the principles of the Sunnah – the practices, sayings, and teachings of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). These teachings form the foundation of our approach to Qur'anic education, ensuring that our students not only learn the Qur'an but also embody the spirit of its teachings in their lives.

Our Approach to Learning

At Ma'hd-ush-Shaatibiy Qur'an Academy, we understand that learning the Qur'an is not just about memorizing words; it is about internalizing its meaning, reflecting on its lessons, and applying its teachings in our daily lives. We encourage our students to develop a deep connection with the Qur'an, not just as a text but as a source of guidance, peace, and strength.

Core Values

  • Respect for Knowledge: We hold the Qur'an and the Sunnah in the highest regard, understanding that knowledge is a gift from Allah (SWT) and a path to personal and spiritual growth.
  • Empowerment Through Education: We believe in empowering women with knowledge that transforms their lives, providing them with the tools to become stronger, more confident, and spiritually enriched individuals.
  • Personalized Learning: Each student is unique, and so is her learning journey. We offer personalized teaching methods that cater to the specific needs of each student, ensuring they achieve their goals at their own pace.
  • Commitment to Excellence: We are dedicated to providing the highest standard of Qur'anic education, combining traditional methods with modern technology to ensure our students receive an enriching and fulfilling experience.
  • Adherence to Sunnah: Our approach to teaching is inspired by the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), promoting virtues such as kindness, patience, discipline, and love for the Qur'an.

Our Goal

We aim to cultivate a nurturing environment where our students are not only learning the Qur'an but also becoming leaders in their communities, empowered by the knowledge and wisdom of the Qur'an. We believe that education is the key to unlocking the potential within every woman, and by connecting with the Qur'an, women can achieve the highest level of spiritual fulfillment and contribute meaningfully to their families, communities, and society.

Lifelong Learning

At Ma'hd-ush-Shaatibiy Qur'an Academy, we understand that learning the Qur'an is a lifelong journey. Our students are not just learners but part of a growing family of women united by their love for the Qur'an. Whether you're just beginning or have already started your journey, we welcome you to join us and experience the transformative power of the Qur'an.

Closing Blessing

May Allah (SWT) bless all our students with success in their learning and make their journey through the Qur'an a source of peace, guidance, and immense reward. Ameen.
