Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I enroll in the programs?

To enroll in any of our programs, simply visit our Registration Page, fill out the form, and submit it. Once your registration is complete, our team will contact you with the next steps.

2. What programs do you offer?

We offer the following programs:

  • Beginner's Qur'an Recitation Course
  • Hifdh (Memorization) Program
  • Makhaarij (Articulation Points) Program
  • Tajweed Program
  • Qaa'idah (Arabic Reading and Recitation Rules) Program

3. How long is each program?

Our programs are self-paced, allowing you to learn at your own speed. The duration depends on your availability, dedication, and personal pace, but the teacher will provide personalised guidance to ensure progress.

4. Do I need prior knowledge of the Qur'an to join the programs?

No, you don't need any prior knowledge to join our programs. We welcome students of all levels, from beginners to advanced learners. Our teachers will guide you according to your current level and help you progress step by step.

5. How are the classes conducted?

All our classes are conducted online, which means you can learn from the comfort of your home. The schedule is flexible, and lessons are tailored to suit your individual needs. You will have one-on-one sessions with your teacher to ensure personalised learning.

6. Do I need to attend classes at a fixed time?

No, our classes are flexible and personalised. While you will have a schedule agreed upon with your teacher, the exact timing of your sessions can be arranged to fit your convenience.

7. What is the cost of the programs?

The pricing for our programs varies depending on the course and the duration. We offer competitive pricing with an option for a free trial session to help you get started. Please contact us for detailed pricing information or check our Programs Page.

8. How do I pay for the program?

We offer flexible payment options, including bank transfers, PayPal, and other online payment methods. Once you register, we will provide further details on how to complete the payment process.

9. Can I get a certificate after completing a program?

Yes! After successfully completing a program, you will receive a certificate of completion. This certificate acknowledges your hard work and dedication to learning the Qur'an.

10. What if I have additional questions?

If you have any other questions or need further assistance, please feel free to contact us via our Contact Page, or email us directly at support@aaishaquranacademy.com. We are happy to help!
